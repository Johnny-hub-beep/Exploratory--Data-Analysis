Conclusion

This Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) provided a comprehensive understanding of the sales dataset by examining its structure, quality, and key characteristics. Initial exploration helped identify variable types, dataset size, and potential data quality issues such as missing values and outliers.

Through descriptive statistics and visual analysis, meaningful trends and patterns were identified across numerical and categorical variables. Visualization techniques such as histograms, boxplots, and grouped comparisons revealed distribution behavior, anomalies, and differences in sales performance across categories.

Statistical analysis and hypothesis testing were used to validate assumptions about relationships between variables. Correlation analysis and scatter plots supported evidence-based conclusions, highlighting relationships that may influence business performance.

Overall, this EDA established a strong foundation for further analysis or predictive modeling by ensuring data readiness, uncovering actionable insights, and identifying areas requiring additional preprocessing.
